Welcome to the CE U11 Basketball Website!

The CE U11 Basketball Team is a group of talented and passionate young players who are dedicated to learning and improving their basketball skills. Our team consists of boys aged 10 and 11, coming from various schools in the community.

This website serves as a central hub for all team-related information, including roster details, game statistics, and upcoming events.

We invite you to explore our site, support our players, and stay updated on our journey throughout the season.
